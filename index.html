About Me.

As a current Computer Science student and an entrepreneur, I love creating new projects.
My latest project is called "InstaData", an open-source software tool that enables the users
to gain more access on their Instagram statistics. This application analyzes data pulled from
Instagram servers and provides the users with meaningful outcome. It embraces many features,
including a list and count of current followers, differences in follow and unfollows compared
to the last time the app is used, profiles who do not follow the user back, and a graph window
that visually represents the growth or decline of the user's follower count.
